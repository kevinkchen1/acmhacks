Explore UCSC Fitness Opportunities

UCSC Fitlife

These are classes that are offered to all UCSC Athletics & Recreation Members, including students, staff and faculty! Classes include Pilates, Yoga, Zumba and many more!

Fitness Center

UCSC Fitness Center has a vast range of equipment to help you reach your fitness goals. They have a complete set of all the weighlifting equipment you need on the first floor along with a large section on the upper floor for cardio!

Cardio

Indoor Cycling Class is one of the exercises that is offered in the Fitlife Program! Indoor Cycling is an excellent form of cardio which is good for cutting down body fat and overall weight.
